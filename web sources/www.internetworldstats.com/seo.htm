Search Engine Optimization
Search engine optimization (SEO) is the process of designing,
writing, coding, programming, and scripting your entire Website
so that there is a good chance that your Web pages will appear
at the top of search engine queries for your selected keywords.

››› SEARCH ENGINE OPTIMIZATION - SEO

Considering that search engine optimization requires ongoing updating of
Web sites, it makes sense that companies would spend money on their
sites -- and invest in agencies, including search agencies, that have
expertise with ranking high in the search engines. The other option
is to "do it yourself" (DIY). This requires SEO learning, coaching
and use of analytic and tracking tools.
