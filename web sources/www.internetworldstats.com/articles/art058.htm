Free Software Review 2006
 By Enrique De Argaez, MBA, P.E., webmaster

In the Internet there are some good programs available for free. Freeware as they are called, mostly correspond to the open source software. One word of WARNING to the Internet beginners. You should be cautious and investigate BEFORE downloading the free programs. They might contain spyware and adware hidden in them. This will affect your computer performance and your privacy.

Below are some recommended freeware and shareware programs that have been tested and used by me and have been found safe as well as useful. They are freeware at the present time:

 Firefox browser
The new safe browser from Mozilla, free source code that is now available in 19 languages, for Windows, MacOS X and Linux. Get Thunderbird the email program that is also free.

 Free Autobot
This free program automatically sends e-mails to everyone who requests further information from your e-mail marketing campaign, web site or banner-advert within seconds. Then, FreeAutoBot posts an continuous series of follow-up e-mails to each of these leads. The e-mails are sent - each day, week, month or any combination thereof - as you decide for your emarketing campaign.

 Good Keywords
Good Keywords is a free Windows software for finding the perfect set of keywords for your web pages. Knowing the right keywords to target is very important because your site should provide what people are searching for. You can also use Good Keywords as a meta tag creation tool. Keep adding the keywords you need into the Keyword Pad available within the software. Once you are done, a simple click will get you the required keyword meta tag ready to be pasted into your HTML file.

 HTML-Kit
Editor program for building HTML web pages.

 Irfanview
Excellent for processing images and graphics.

 Opera browser
Another fast and safe web browser.

 SiteUptime
Free website monitoring service that watches your website 24/7/365 and notifies you if it becomes unavailable.

 Spybot
A very good spyware destroyer.

 Statcounter
A free yet reliable invisible web tracker, highly configurable hit counter and real-time detailed web stats. Insert a simple piece of our code on your web page and you will be able to analyse and monitor all the visitors to your website in real-time!

 Thunderbird
The new email program from the Mozilla Foundation. Makes a great team with the Firefox browser.


For those of you who would like to explore more freeware and shareware programs, check the following web sites:

 Freeware Arena this website contains news and links on freeware. It is in redesign right now. It features the Freeware Book Project (download).

New free programs will be added as time allows. Bookmark this article and come back frequently.
