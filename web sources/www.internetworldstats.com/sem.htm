Search Engine Marketing
Search Engine Marketing and its acronym SEM refer to the methods of driving
highly targeted visitors to your web site. SEM is much more than SEO. Search
engine optimization (SEO) is actually only one type of SEM. Another
major type of SEM would be pay-per-click advertising (PPC).
