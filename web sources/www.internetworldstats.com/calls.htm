How does IP Telephony (VoIP) work

VoIP allows anyone using the Internet, with a PC having a sound card, a speaker and microphone, iniciate calls from his computer to reach far away phones or another Computer...at lower rates.

 VoIP Benefits

VoIP integrates the global communications through the Internet: you may communicate for free from PC to PC. Likewise you may benefit from important reductions in your phone bill of up to 90% regarding traditional long distance calls.

All you need is to download a free software for calling from a PC to a regular phone, and to purchase some calling time.
Requirements for installing a VoIP telephony system
  • Windows 98 or newer, Windows NT 4.0+
  • PC Pentium, 166 MHz or higher
  • 32 MB RAM
  • Modem 56 kbps
  • Internet PPP connection
  • Full Duplex sound card
  • Microphone
  • Speakers
  • Accesories are optional
