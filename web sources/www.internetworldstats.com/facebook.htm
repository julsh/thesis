More Internet Information Sources About Facebook
  • Wikipedia
    Facebook description in the Wikipedia, history, creation, growth, founders, users and other basic data.

  • Twitter Facebook Page
    The Twitter Facebook Page, with 335,711 followers and in 11,584 lists, as of September 12, 2010. You can get updates from Facebook here.

  • Facebook in Alexa
    According to Alexa, Facebook is one of the top ten websites in the world. Facebook.com has rank 2 in Alexa, Google.com has rank 1, YouTube.com has rank 3.
  • PEW Research Center Publications
    Older Adults and Social Media. Social networking use among internet users ages 50 and older has nearly doubled, from 22% to 42% over the past year. While social media use has grown dramatically across all age groups, older users have been especially enthusiastic over the past year about embracing new networking tools.

  • Check Facebook
    Each day, CheckFacebook.com tracks data reported from Facebook's advertising tool to help marketers and researchers understand how Facebook is spreading across the globe.

  • Nielsen Wire Blog
    Statistics and trends in US Top Social Network Sites 2008, 2009, 2010 Year over Year Gains. Compared to user activity on social networking sites last year, Facebook and Twitter posted gains of 69% and 45% respectively in the U.S.
  • Facebook Blog
    Find out what's happening behind the scenes at Facebook. Facebook employees give first hand accounts of new features, products, and goings-on at the Facebook blog.

  • Famecount - Top Facebook Pages
     Famecount is an independent website, not affiliated with Facebook, Youtube, Twitter nor other services. They provide statistics data updated daily.

  • Comscore Facebook Asia and Pacific Area Report
     Comscore found that 50.8 percent of the total online population in the Asia-Pacific region visited a social networking site in February 2010, reaching a total of 240.3 million visitors. Facebook.com ranked as the top social network across the majority of individual markets in the region, while competing brands commanded the top position in certain markets, including Orkut in India, Mixi.jp in Japan, CyWorld in South Korea and Wretch.cc in Taiwan.

  • Discover Facebook Popular Pages
     Facebook has just launched a new way to "Discover Facebook's Popular Pages" called Browser. It shows icons of Pages that are popular in a user's country, but factors in which Pages which are popular amongst their unique friend network. When the Page icons are hovered over they display a Like Button. Browser also shows a "Friends similar to you" leaderboard displaying with which friends you share the most mutual Likes. It's unclear how users can navigate to the utility other than through the URL.

  • ZYNGA
    Zynga is a Web 2.0-based social network game developer located in San Francisco, California, United States. The company develops browser-based games that work both stand-alone and as application widgets on social networking websites such as Facebook and MySpace. Zynga games include: FarmVille, Mafia Wars, YoVille, FishVille, PetVille, Treasure Isle and Café World.
