Andorra
 Internet Usage Stats and Telecom Reports
Internet Usage Statistics:
 67,200 Internet users as of June/10, 79.5% of the population, according to the ITU.

Facebook Users in Andorra:
23,320 as of August 31, 2010, according to Facebook.

Latest Population Estimate:
84,525 population for 2010, according to US Census Bureau.

Gross Domestic Product:
GDP per capita (ppp) is US$ 42,500 ('07) according to World Bank.

Country Size (Area):
Andorra has 464 sq km - Population density is 182 persons per sq km.

Andorra e-Government Page
Andorra is tackling its digital divide problem from the roots up by encouraging more ICT use in schools across the country. With the support of the United Nations, the Ministry of Education and Science is putting together plans for a new eSchool programme giving primary and secondary students in much-needed internet and IT skills and knowledge.
