Editorial

The end of year 2006 is very near, and it is time to wish you all the best Season's Greetings. After the holidays, the Newsletter will be back with new estimates and information. There is not much else to report.

In the next issue we will give the 2006 year-end statistics, and a growth comparison by geographic world region. The current number of Internet users is in the 1,100 million range.

Happy holidays to all and our best wishes for a prosperous and peaceful New Year 2007.
