A Free Tip for Search Engine Success:

1 - Content is King - add new content and/or new products to satisfy your visitors needs. Update always. Take time to review pages to ensure that they at least have unique, keyword-rich title and Meta tags. Use a metatag checker.

2 - Keyword density is important. Get density right with online Tools.

3 - The right Linking is good for your page rank and for generating traffic. Keep in good company and link with the right sites. Check your link popularity with the online Checkers.

5 - Select the right Keywords in line with your purposes and goals.

5 - Don't fall for all the hype you read about SEO. Most is not true.

››› CHECKING KEYWORD DENSITY

SEO Tools and Tips
SearchEngineWorld
 A great keyword density checker. Simply tell this little "spider simulator"
which of your pages to check and it will analyze your content and let you
know which search terms are the most repeated on that page. Make sure
that you have between 3-5 search terms targeted on each page and aim for
density between 5-15% (depending on the number of total words for that page).

MarketLeap
 Free online tool to check three important factors for your web pages:
your link popularity, search engine saturation and keyword verification.

 Keyword Counter
This is a really handy tool to find out why your competitors are getting
better search engine rankings than you are. A great little tool if you are
wondering why your rankings are so low. And the price is RIGHT.
