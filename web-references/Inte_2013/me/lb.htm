Lebanon Telecommunications Market Overview & Statistics Report
This report provides an overview of the telecommunications market in Lebanon, accompanied by relevant statistics. Developments in the market have been on hold for some years but the political changes happening at present may perhaps release the logjam. Efforts at privatisation and the establishment of an independent regulator have been delayed in the implementation phase. Some competition exists in the data and Internet Service Provision market but the introduction of DSL broadband services has been imminent for a considerable time. Mobile networks are government owned.
