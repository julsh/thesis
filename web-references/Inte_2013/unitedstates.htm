United States
United States of America is the third country in the world by land size
(after Russia and Canada) located in the North American continent and
bordered to the west by the Pacific Ocean and to the east by the Atlantic Ocean, between Canada and Mexico. The USA is divided into 50 states
and one district. It also includes 14 dependent areas or territories.
