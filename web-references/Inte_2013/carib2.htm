Caribbean Telecommunications Union
The Caribbean Telecommunications Union (CTU) is a Caribbean
organisation dedicated to facilitating the development of the regional
telecommunications sector. Membership includes Caribbean States,
private sector organizations and non-governmental agencies.

Caribbean Telecommunications Markets General Overview and Statistics
Despite being relatively small markets by global standards, one of the Caribbean’s major growth industries is telecommunications. Whilst in a handful of countries Cable and Wireless still holds a monopoly in the fixed-line sector, many countries now have other operators offering fixed-line services at competitive prices. The region’s mobile subscriber base has been witnessing significant growth in recent years driven, in particular, by aggressive competition from Digicel. This report provides an overview of the Caribbean’s telecom sector accompanied by relevant statistics and a brief profile of the major players.
