Albania e-Government Page
Albania is tackling its digital divide problem from the roots up by encouraging more ICT use in schools across the country. With the support of the United Nations, the Albanian Ministry of Education and Science is putting together plans for a new eSchool programme giving primary and secondary students in Albania much-needed internet and IT skills and knowledge.
