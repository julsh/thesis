Romania Telecommunications Market Reports

 Romania Convergence, Broadband & Internet Market
- Overview, Statistics & Forecasts
Romania’s Internet and broadband market is experiencing strong growth, despite the country’s low fixed-line penetration rate. Broadband services are widely available from the cable operators as well as the fixed-line incumbent, RomTelecom, which has made up for belatedly launched ADSL services through aggressively pricing its services. Large LAN-based broadband services are also popular. Triple play services have been launched by the cable operators, which pose a significant threat to RomTelecom given the reach of their networks. A number of satellite-based digital pay TV platforms were launched during 2005, with one operator expanding services internationally. Digital terrestrial TV services are still in development although an analogue switch-off date has been approved and developments are continuing. This report provides an insight into the country’s burgeoning Internet, broadband and convergence market, covering the technologies, the major players and market developments, in addition to a variety of statistics and forecasts on the future broadband penetration. Romania Key Statistics, Regulatory & Fixed Line Telecoms Overviews
Romania’s telecommunications market is in transition, reflecting the country’s economic aspirations. The regulatory environment has been shaped by the country’s bid to join the European Union, resulting in a liberalised market and plans to privatise the government’s remaining stake in the incumbent. Alternative operators have made significant progress in the fixed-line market, with the country’s cable operators playing a significant role. Significant infrastructure investments have been made in the fixed-line network, spurred by the need to offer broadband services and increase fixed-line network access. This report provides an insight into the burgeoning telecommunications market, covering regulatory developments, fixed-line infrastructure and offering a variety of statistics. See the summary and table of contents


Romania - Mobile Market - Overview & Statistics

Romania’s mobile communications market is served by three GSM and one CDMA operator, although the market is effectively a duopoly of two GSM operators. However, the re-emergence of the third operator looks set to inject fresh competition into the market. Mobile data and content services are available and the launch of 3G services by two operators has enabled the availability of increasingly sophisticated mobile content. A beauty contest-style tender for the country’s third and fourth 3G licences has been completed, with the CDMA operator and a fixed-line/cable TV operator emerging as the winners. This report provides a concise overview of Romania’s burgeoning mobile market, covering and analysing the market and regulatory developments, as well as profiling the major operators and offering a variety of financial and performance statistics.
