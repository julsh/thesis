  • Asia Mobile, Broadband and Digital Economy
    Asia represents 49% of the regions mobile subscribers and broadband markets.
    This massive presence is due to the fact that Asia has 56% of the world population
    as well as 45% of the Internet Users, making Asia clearly a top leader in the world
    telecommunications scenario.

    Following a decade of strong growth, mobile, broadband and the digital economy
    of the Asian region, the number of mobile users is expected to reach 3.2 billion
    subscribers at the end of 2012. Some 18 countries in Asia had over 100% mobile
    penetration, and two (Macau and Hong Kong) had impressive penetration levels of
    more than 200%. Major mobile growth areas are value added services and mobile
    broadband. India and China still have room for expansion, even though these two
    countries together account for over 60% of the market share. Read more here    .
