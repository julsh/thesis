Internet Usage Update for Q1-2005

Internet World Stats has updated its statistics to 888 million Internet users as of March, 2005. This figure corresponds to a global penetration rate of 13.9%, up from previous 12.7% worldwide penetration at the end of 2004.

The largest increase in Internet usage corresponds to Asia with 36 million users, next is Europe with 29 million. Smaller usage increases were observed for the Middle East with 2 million and the Americas with 4 million users. All the statistics tables have been updated and the layout improved with alternating colored lines. Check them out at world stats.

Google's Define Feature

Search Engines are very important to everyday Internet use. In their effort to provide more and better services, SE keep adding features and additional services. For example, everybody knows that the Google search window can be used as a powerful calculator. But other features are not so well known.

Google can give you the definition for a word or a phrase in English. To use this feature, simply type the word "define," then a space, and then the word(s) you want defined. If Google has seen a definition for the word or phrase on the Web, it will retrieve that information and display it at the top of your search results page.

You can also get a list of only definitions for a word by including the special operator "define:" with no space between it and the term you want defined. For example, the search [define:statistics] will show you a list of definitions for "statistics" gathered from various online sources by Google.

The two methods work very well for defining words in English. Both methods are useful, the results obtained are different of course.
