ICRA - Internet Content Rating Association
ICRA works with a variety of organizations around the world which, in
one way or another help to promote the protection of children from
potentially harmful content, and protect free speech on the Internet.

 ICANN
Internet Corporation For Assigned Names and Numbers (ICANN) is the private
sector, non-profit corporation created in 1998 to assume the responsibility for
managing and coordinating the Domain Name System (DNS) to ensure that every
address is unique and that Internet users can find all the valid addresses.

 ISOC - Internet Society
The Internet Society is a non-profit, non-governmental, international,
professional membership organization. Its more than 100 organization
members and over 20,000 individual members in over 180 nations
worldwide represent a veritable who's who of the Internet community.
