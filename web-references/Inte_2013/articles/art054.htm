How to Start Your Own Web Design Internet Business

The growing demand for affordable Web sites and able Webmasters, especially in the Small Small Business (SSB) market, is encouraging more and more Webmasters to strike out on their own and set up a home-based business. There is only one drawback for most of them... their "business" skills are not as highly developed as their design skills. These Webmasters are savvy enough to know that creativity is only one piece of the success puzzle...

The Webmaster BUSINESS Masters Course is a free e-book that provides the information and resources needed to start (and keep) your Webmastering business on solid footing. It's written by an independent designer, Mark Frank, who generously shares his business knowledge, first-hand experiences and tips.

The Webmaster BUSINESS Masters Course won’t teach you how to design Web sites or help you layout pages or show you any cute HTML tricks. You must already have that under control or you wouldn’t be getting ready to hang out your business shingle.

The Webmaster BUSINESS Masters Course will, however, help you to establish your business on solid footing and position yourself as a successful independent designer.
